Our Mission

To engineer highly resilient electrical distribution systems that eliminate carbon combustion dependancies. We align advanced utility-scale photovoltaic, atmospheric wind turbine, and battery storage components to build intelligent grid-connected nodes.

By deploying local power aggregation grids, we bypass legacy centralized grid vulnerabilities, guaranteeing 99.99% system availability to urban zones, business estates, and agricultural hubs alike.

Our Progress

Key Milestones

Our journey of integrating advanced engineering into ecological infrastructure.

2019

Lab Testing & Formation

Founded by solar cell researchers in Silicon Valley, experimenting with microgrid battery controllers and power balancing software interfaces.

2021

First Municipal Sizing Contract

Secured contract to scale microgrid grids across five public municipalities in California, totaling 12MW of connected photovoltaic arrays.

2023

Global Grid Expansion

Opened EMEA and APAC operations. Deployed large off-grid wind farms in northern Europe, shifting our total capacity past 300MW.

2026

Smart AI Grid 2.0

Released decentralized routing node software utilizing predictive machine learning model to stabilize solar grid outputs during bad weather patterns.
